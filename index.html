Flagship Campaign · Brand Strategy

Health Kundli — "From Janam Patri to Health Prediction"

Amrita Hospital · Genomics Awareness Campaign

Conceived and developed a culturally intelligent experiential marketing campaign that reframes genomics through the familiar metaphor of a traditional Kundli chart. The campaign bridges the communication disconnect around preventive healthcare by positioning DNA testing as "scientific health forecasting" — making complex genomics emotionally understandable to mass Indian audiences.

Core insight: Families trust Kundli charts for life decisions but ignore genetic predispositions — bridging this gap through cultural metaphor
Execution model across RWAs (Sunday health camps), Malls (health kiosks), and Corporates ("Corporate Health Horoscope")
3-step on-ground experience: Curiosity trigger → visual explanation → soft DNA test registration
Pilot proposal: 2 RWAs, 1 Mall, 1 Corporate Park — measuring engagement and conversion psychology
Positioned as brand-building + health education initiative; non-fear-based, non-clinical in tone
